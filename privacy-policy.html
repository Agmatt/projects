Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to the Inuana Community Action Network (ICAN), a community organization based in Homa Bay Town, Homa Bay County, Kenya. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency in how we handle your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your data when you visit our website or engage with our programs.

What Data We Collect

We may collect the following personal information:

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number, or address provided through membership forms, event registrations, or contact forms.

  • Website Usage Data: Information about how you use our website, such as IP addresses, browser type, and pages visited, collected via cookies or analytics tools like Google Analytics.

  • Voluntary Information: Feedback, survey responses, or messages you send us.

How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Manage memberships and send updates about ICAN’s programs and events.

  • Respond to your inquiries or feedback.

  • Improve our website and services through analytics.

  • Comply with legal obligations under Kenya’s Data Protection Act, 2019.

Sharing Your Data

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties except:

  • Service Providers: Trusted third parties like email providers (e.g., Mailchimp) or analytics tools (e.g., Google Analytics) that help us operate our website and programs. These providers are bound by contracts to protect your data.

  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect ICAN’s rights.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data, including encryption, secure storage, and access controls. However, no online system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

Under Kenya’s Data Protection Act, 2019, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data we hold.

  • Request correction or deletion of your data.

  • Object to or restrict the processing of your data.

  • Request data portability.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the updated policy on our website with a new “Last updated” date.
