Inuana Community Action Network (ICAN)

INUANA COMMUNITY ACTION NETWORK (ICAN) is a Community Based Organization founded by active, dedicated and committed youths in 2023 and fully registered under Civil Society of Kenya within the State Department for Social Protection.

ICAN has a membership of over 30 members as of 2024 which comprises of students, freelancers, healthcare workers and social workers working in Siaya, Kisumu and Homabay Counties in Kenya but all focused on health and health related issues. The various members have diverse health-related interests but all share on the vision of having healthy Kenyan communities.

ICAN aims to improve community wellbeing, address health, economic, and social challenges through innovative programs and partnerships.

Our History

In the vibrant heart of Homa Bay Town, Homa Bay County, Kenya, a group of passionate youths ignited a movement for change. As the Inuana Community Organization, they poured their hearts into uplifting lives through health, youth empowerment, and unity, determined to transform their communityâ€™s future.

A pivotal moment arrived when they met three like-minded individuals, each bringing unique expertise and a shared dream of lasting impact, but no platform to act on it. This meeting sparked a powerful alliance, uniting grassroots passion with fresh perspectives.

From this collaboration, the Inuana Community Organization transformed into the Inuana Community Action Network (ICAN), officially registered as a legal entity with a renewed mission. Now a vibrant community of at least 30 dedicated members, ICAN stands stronger than ever, driven to empower youth, enhance health outcomes, and build a brighter, more inclusive future for all.
