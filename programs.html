Our Programs

Empowering our community through impactful initiatives

Greening Our Future

Our planet needs us. Through tree-planting drives and community cleanups, we’re restoring local ecosystems. In 2023, we planted 50 native trees, creating habitats for wildlife and cleaner air.

We also educate schools and families about recycling and sustainable practices. These efforts ensure a healthier environment for future generations.

Charity Works

We believe in fostering dignity and hope. Our programs connect neighbors, encouraging community members to give back. By working together, we’re not just addressing immediate needs but building a stronger, more resilient community.

Join us at our next donation drive and see the impact of collective kindness firsthand.

Health projects

Access to healthcare is a right, not a privilege. Our health projects bring free medical camps, vaccination drives, and wellness workshops to underserved areas.

We partner with local clinics and volunteers to deliver quality care and education. From blood pressure screenings to nutrition classes, we empower our community to live healthier lives.

Volunteer at our next health camp and help us make wellness accessible to everyone.

Empowering Livelihood

Financial independence transforms lives. Our income-generating projects provide skills training and microfinance to help individuals start small businesses. We vision to support over 50 entrepreneurs.

We offer workshops on budgeting and marketing, ensuring long-term success. These initiatives not only boost incomes but also strengthen community bonds.

Support our training session and help someone build a brighter future.

Envisioning a future where people in rural settings flourish and providing diverse resources that promote self sufficiency and foster community and economic development

Tracking Our Progress

We believe in transparency and accountability. Our impact tracking system meticulously monitors and evaluates the outcomes of our programs. Through detailed reports and success stories, we showcase how our efforts have transformed communities. We measure success by improved health statistics, economic growth, and enhanced social cohesion within the communities we serve.

Together we can build a great community

Population Coverage

At Inuana Community Action Network (ICAN), we are dedicated to reaching every corner of the rural communities in Kenya especially Homabay County with our headquarters at Homabay Town. Our initiatives have positively impacted lives by promoting health, economic stability, and social well-being. We aim to expand our reach and touch even more lives, fostering a healthier, more prosperous, and empowered rural Kenya.
